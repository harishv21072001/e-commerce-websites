About Us

Flipkart is an e-commerce company founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. The company owns and operates an online shopping website in India. Flipkart, registered in Singapore, has also launched its own product range under the name "DigiFlip" with products including tablets, USBs, and laptop bags.Flipkart now offers stocking and shipping service to the merchants selling their products on Flipkart. For the Sellers: They will need to stock their products at Flipkart's warehouses even before the orders are placed.
